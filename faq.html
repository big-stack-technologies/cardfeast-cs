FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a transaction take?

Most gift card transactions take less than 10 minutes. However, trades are attended to on a first-in-first-out basis. Also, there are some gift cards that take longer time to process, within 30 mins to 3 hours, these are classified as store cards e.g Apple Store, Amex, Best Buy, eBay, Nordstrom, Nike, Sephora, Vanilla, Target, Walmart e.t.c

How to withdraw money from my wallet?

The amount for every successful transaction is added to your cardfeast wallet, which you can withdraw at any time. Follow this procedure to withdraw your funds:

  • Kindly login to the app.
  • Click on Withdraw funds on your App’s dashboard.
  • Enter your account number.
  • Choose a bank from the drop-down list and your account name would be displayed automatically.
  • Click on Withdraw.
Note: The minimum amount you can withdraw is ₦1000.

How to check rates and sell gift cards?

The process is very easy:

  • Kindly login to the app.
  • Click on Trade Gift Cards on the dashboard.
  • Select the type of card from the Gift Card Category
  • Select the Sub-category of the gift card from the drop-down list. For instance: If you have a USA Physical Steam gift card of $50, you should select “Steam.” from the drop-down list while the sub-category should be USA Steam Physical. If you have a USA Steam E-code of $50, you should select “Steam.” from the drop-down list while the sub-category should be USA Steam E-code.
  • Enter the Total amount(s) of the cards in the space provided, the rate at which the gift card(s) would be processed would also be displayed under the “Rate” column.
  • Click "Upload gift card images(s)” and select the gift card image from your gallery.
  • You can input your codes in the comments section provided.
  • Click on Trade.
It's that easy. If you need more help, contact our customer support team at support@cardfeast.com or 09160006505

Why is my transaction pending?

The ‘pending’ status indicates that your trade is currently being attended to by the admin and will be concluded soon. Kindly be patient, you will receive a notification when the status is updated. Kindly understand that no trade is neglected or intentionally left unattended to. All transactions are important and are attended to in FIFO (First-In-First-Out) order.

What happens if my gift card is rejected?

If your gift card is rejected due to an incorrect balance, ineligibility, or any other reason, we will notify you immediately. In some cases, you may have the option to resubmit the card with corrected information.

Can I cancel a transaction?

Once you have accepted our offer and the payment process has begun, the transaction cannot be cancelled. Please ensure that you are certain before accepting any offer.

Why was my withdrawal rejected?

We offer a seamless withdrawal process and you should have no issue withdrawing your funds. However, there are cases where withdrawals are rejected which is mostly due to unavailable banks. Meanwhile, have no worries, if your withdrawal is rejected, the amount goes back to your cardfeast wallet immediately.

Difference between e-code And physical card?

Physical gift cards: These are actual physical (plastic) cards that are purchased directly from a physical store. To trade this on Cardfeast, you have to scratch the card and upload an image.

E-codes: These are digital gift codes purchased online and are mostly sent to the buyer’s email address or gotten as paper codes. To trade this on Cardfeast, you either have to upload a screenshot of the codes gotten from your mail, upload the paper codes or write the code in the comment section.

What does it mean when my gift card is not activated?

Gift card providers have adopted the requirement of gift cards to be activated before they can be used as a technique to reduce gift card theft. When you purchase a gift card at the store, it will be activated for you after payment. E-codes are also activated subsequent to purchase. But in cases when your gift card is not purchased through the right channel or is not properly activated at the store, it will not work. However, it is easy to fix. All you need to do is go back to the store where you bought it and complain. Unfortunately, Cardfeast does not accept such cards and we cannot fix the problem.

Google Play Gift Card "Need More Info" Error?

Google Play gift cards generally have this error. The reason behind this is yet to be totally verified. Unfortunately, once a gift card gives this “We Need More Info To Redeem This Card” error, there’s nothing Cardfeast can do to rectify it because the gift card cannot be used. Note that this error does not necessarily mean your gift card has been redeemed. Kindly contact your source, or go back to the store where the gift card was purchased to complain about this situation, the gift card may be replaced. Or CLICK HERE TO CONTACT GOOGLE PLAY SUPPORT DIRECTLY: https://support.google.com/googleplay/answer/9281737. Please understand that Google errors on google play gift cards do not come from us but from google play.

What happens if I upload cards in the wrong category/subcategory?

The transaction will be moved to the right category/subcategory and treated using the rate of the right category.

Yes, it is. Your funds cannot be withdrawn to a bank account that does not carry your full name as registered on your cardfeast account. You can also ensure no one has access to your account and set strict passwords when using the app.
